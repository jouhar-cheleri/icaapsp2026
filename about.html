Partner Society

Ramanujan Mathematical Society (RMS)

The Ramanujan Mathematical Society is one of India's premier professional organizations dedicated to the promotion and advancement of mathematical sciences. Established in 1985 and named in honour of the legendary Indian mathematician Srinivasa Ramanujan, the Society aims to foster mathematical research, education, and outreach activities across the country.

It serves as an important platform for mathematicians, researchers, teachers, and students to exchange ideas and promote excellence in mathematical sciences, supporting research through conferences, workshops, instructional schools, and lectures, and publishing internationally recognized journals.

The Society is committed to nurturing young mathematical talent and strengthening the mathematical community by encouraging collaboration among researchers and institutions, upholding the rich legacy of Srinivasa Ramanujan.

Partner Association

Kerala Mathematical Association (KMA)

The Kerala Mathematical Association is a distinguished organization established in 1962 with the mission of serving the mathematical community — students, teachers, and researchers within Kerala and beyond. With a membership of around one thousand, nearly half of them life members and about three hundred from outside Kerala and abroad, the KMA has a significant and wide-reaching presence.

The Association regularly organizes ten to twelve national and international workshops and seminars each year across Kerala and publishes their proceedings. A key publication is the Bulletin of the Kerala Mathematical Association, an international journal launched in 2004 that publishes original research on mathematics and its applications, releasing two issues per year.

A notable tradition is the annual Prof. T. A. Sarasvati Amma Memorial Lecture, a regular feature of its annual conference since 2002, honouring a pioneering woman mathematician from Kerala.

The Host

About the College

Korambayil Ahamed Haji Memorial Unity Women's College, Manjeri — commonly known as KAHM Unity Women's College — was established in 1991. It is a first-grade institution affiliated to the University of Calicut, Kerala, India. A premier institution dedicated to women's education, the college strives for academic excellence and holistic development, empowering young women through quality education and fostering research, innovation, and interdisciplinary collaboration.

The Organizer

About the Department of Mathematics

The Department of Mathematics at KAHM Unity Women's College was started in 1993. Since its inception, the department has grown steadily and currently offers a Four-Year Honours degree in Mathematics under the University of Calicut. Known for its vibrant academic environment, the department regularly organizes workshops, seminars, and conferences to promote research in applied probability, stochastic processes, and related mathematical fields. Its faculty are dedicated to nurturing young talent and encouraging students to pursue higher studies and research in mathematics.
