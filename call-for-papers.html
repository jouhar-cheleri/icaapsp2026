Call for Papers

Present your research at ICAAP & SP 2026. Selected papers will be considered for a special issue of the Annals of Operations Research (Springer).

Call for Papers

Present your research

We invite researchers, academics, and practitioners to submit their work for presentation at ICAAP & SP 2026. Authors must present their papers at the conference.

Email Your Abstract

Submission is by email. Please attach your two-page extended abstract.

For Submissions

A two-page extended abstract for the presentation may be sent to:

icaapsp2026@unitywomenscollege.ac.in

Mark Your Calendar

Submission deadlines

  • 01 July 2026Paper submission opens
  • 15 November 2026Paper submission closes
  • Within 10 daysNotification of acceptance (from submission)
  • 19–21 Dec 2026Conference dates

Publication

Selected papers, after undergoing a strict review and editorial process in accordance with the journal's policies, will be published in a special issue of the Annals of Operations Research (ANOR) — a premier peer-reviewed journal published by Springer.

Participation

Mode of participation

👥

Participants from within India shall attend the conference in physical mode. Foreign participants have the option of presenting papers through online or offline modes.
