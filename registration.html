Registration & Call for Papers

Everything you need to participate, present your research, and register for ICAAP & SP 2026.

Call for Papers

Present your research

We invite researchers, academics, and practitioners to submit their work for presentation at ICAAP & SP 2026. Selected papers, after undergoing a strict review and editorial process in accordance with the journal's policies, will be published in a special issue of the Annals of Operations Research (ANOR) — a premier peer-reviewed journal published by Springer.

Submit Your Paper

The submission form link will be activated soon.

For Submissions

A two-page extended abstract for the presentation may be sent to:

icaapsp2026@unitywomenscollege.ac.in

Topics for contributory paper presentation

The scope covers all topics in Applied Probability and Stochastic Processes. The main topics of discussion are:

Queueing theory applied to telecommunication and computer networks
Queueing Inventory, including Production Inventory with positive service time
Reliability
Classical Inventory
Coupling of Random Variables and Processes
Insurance and Risk
Theory of Large Deviation
Stochastic Calculus
Stochastic Control
Markov and Semi-Markov Decision Processes
