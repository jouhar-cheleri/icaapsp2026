About the Conference

A platform for the latest in probability & stochastics

Advances in Applied Probability and Stochastic Processes concern the progress made in probability and stochastic processes, particularly in their practical applications across engineering, finance, insurance, telecommunications, and computer science. Recent advances include new mathematical techniques, algorithms, and models that enable more accurate and efficient prediction of probabilistic and stochastic phenomena.

ICAAP & SP 2026 provides a platform for researchers, academics, and practitioners to discuss recent developments and share their findings. The conference features keynote addresses, plenary talks, and invited talks, fostering networking and collaboration among participants from different countries and backgrounds.

Scope

Topics to be covered

Topics include, but are not limited to:

  • Advancement in Pure and Applied Probability
  • Progress in Statistics
  • Development in Stochastic Processes
  • Growth of Stochastic Analysis and its Applications
  • Applications in Network Modelling, Queuing, and Inventory Systems
  • Advances in Operations Research
  • Improvement in Time Series Analysis
  • Probabilistic Methods in Machine Learning and Data Analysis
  • Development of Monte Carlo Simulation
  • Advances in Risk Management and Optimization in Stochastic Systems
  • Progress in Stochastic Differential Equations
  • Improved Uncertainty Modelling using Fuzzy, Soft & Rough Sets
  • Advances in Statistical and Topological Data Analysis Techniques
  • Modelling using Graphs and Hypergraphs
  • Applications in Real-World Fields
  • Advancement in Computational Technologies
  • New Mathematical Techniques and Algorithms
  • Improvement of Existing Applications & Creation of New Ones

Getting Here

How to reach KAHM Unity Women's College (Autonomous), Manjeri

✈️ By Flight

Nearest airport: Calicut International Airport (CCJ), approximately 22 km away. Take a taxi or auto to the college.

Suggested route: Airport → Kondotty → Manjeri Road → Manjeri Town → Narukara → KAHM Unity Women's College (Autonomous).

🚆 By Train

Nearest railway station: Tirur Railway Station. Continue by taxi or bus to Manjeri.

Local buses and taxis are readily available from Manjeri town to the college at Narukara.
